# PHYSIQUEIQ Knowledge Pipeline

## Mission

Every piece of knowledge added to PHYSIQUEIQ must pass through the same pipeline before becoming part of the coaching engine.

The goal is consistency, quality and scientific reliability.

---

## Step 1 — Source Collection

Collect information only from approved scientific and professional sources.

Examples:

- ACSM
- NSCA
- ISSN
- IOC
- PubMed
- Peer-reviewed journals
- Scientific textbooks
- Official federation guidelines

---

## Step 2 — Evidence Validation

Evaluate:

- Study design
- Population
- Sample size
- Limitations
- Evidence level
- Practical relevance

---

## Step 3 — Knowledge Extraction

Extract structured information only.

Never copy entire papers.

Capture:

- Practical recommendations
- Population
- Conditions
- Contraindications
- Key findings
- Coaching implications

---

## Step 4 — Knowledge Object Creation

Convert extracted information into structured objects.

Examples:

Exercise Object

Food Object

Supplement Object

Recovery Object

Research Object

Decision Rule

---

## Step 5 — Quality Review

Verify:

Accuracy

Completeness

Evidence Level

Scientific consistency

Duplicate detection

---

## Step 6 — Decision Engine Integration

Approved knowledge becomes available to:

Workout Generator

Nutrition Generator

Recovery Engine

Weekly Coach

AI Chat

---

## Step 7 — Continuous Review

Knowledge must be reviewed periodically.

New scientific evidence may:

Update

Replace

Expand

Existing knowledge.
