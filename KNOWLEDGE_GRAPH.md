# PHYSIQUEIQ Knowledge Graph

## Vision

The PHYSIQUEIQ Knowledge Graph is the central intelligence map of the platform.

It defines how every piece of knowledge connects to every other piece of knowledge.

The AI does not search text.

The AI navigates relationships.

# Design Principle

Knowledge should exist only once.

Everything else references it.

No duplicated knowledge.

No hardcoded recommendations.
