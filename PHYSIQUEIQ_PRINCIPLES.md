# PHYSIQUEIQ Principles

## Mission

Build the world's most trusted evidence-based AI coaching platform for strength, physique, health and athletic performance.

---

# Principle 1

The user should never need to understand programming.

PHYSIQUEIQ understands programming.

The user understands coaching.

---

# Principle 2

Every recommendation must have a reason.

Nothing is random.

---

# Principle 3

Every recommendation must be explainable.

The platform should always answer:

Why?

---

# Principle 4

Evidence over popularity.

Scientific evidence always outweighs trends.

---

# Principle 5

Safety before performance.

Health is always the highest priority.

---

# Principle 6

Personalization over templates.

Programs are created for people.

Not people for programs.

---

# Principle 7

Knowledge exists only once.

Every object references the central knowledge base.

No duplicated logic.

---

# Principle 8

Separate Knowledge from Decisions.

Knowledge stores facts.

Decision Engine applies facts.

---

# Principle 9

Every recommendation should improve over time.

The system continuously learns from new scientific evidence.

---

# Principle 10

Trust is the product.

Users should trust PHYSIQUEIQ more than any generic AI response.

---

# Long-Term Vision

PHYSIQUEIQ should become the global standard for evidence-based AI coaching.

The platform should be capable of supporting:

- General Fitness
- Bodybuilding
- Powerlifting
- ICN / Contest Prep
- Fat Loss
- Muscle Gain
- Sports Performance
- Injury Prevention
- Rehabilitation
- Longevity
- Women's Health
- Older Adults
- Youth Athletes
- Tactical Athletes

without changing the core architecture.
